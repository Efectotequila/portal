Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 19, 2024

Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how the Random Numbers app ("we", "our", or "us") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our application.

Information We Collect

Our app is designed to be privacy-focused and collects minimal information:

  • App Settings: We store your preferences for minimum and maximum number ranges, and text-to-speech settings locally on your device.
  • Text-to-Speech: When enabled, the app uses your device's text-to-speech capabilities to read numbers aloud. This processing happens entirely on your device.
  • Google Cast: If you choose to use the Google Cast feature, the app will connect to your Cast-enabled devices on your local network to display numbers.

How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information solely for the following purposes:

  • To provide the core functionality of generating random numbers
  • To remember your preferences between app sessions
  • To enable text-to-speech functionality when requested
  • To support Google Cast functionality when enabled

Data Storage

All app settings and preferences are stored locally on your device. We do not:

  • Store any data on external servers
  • Share your information with third parties
  • Track your usage or collect analytics

Permissions

Our app requires the following permissions:

  • Internet: Required for Google Cast functionality
  • Network State: Required to check for Cast device availability

Children's Privacy

Our app is suitable for all ages and does not collect any personal information from children or adults. The app is designed to be simple and safe for everyone to use.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy in the app and updating the "Last updated" date.

Your Rights

Since we don't collect personal information, there is no data to access, modify, or delete. All app settings can be reset by uninstalling and reinstalling the app.
